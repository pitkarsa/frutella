breakpoints:
============

screen width at which the css changes. (in case of responsive design)

Screen sizes:

extra small (xs)      < 576px (upto 575px)
small  (sm)            576 - 767 px
medium (md)           768 - 991px
large  (lg)            992 - 1199px
extra large   (xl)     1200  - 1399px
XXL    (xxl)            >= 1400px
